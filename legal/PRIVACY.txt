TermFlow — Privacy Notice

Version 1.0
Effective date: 2026-07-11
Controller: PMT Labs LLC, Texas, USA
Contact: privacy@termflow.app

TermFlow is a local-first desktop terminal. We designed it to keep your data on
your own machine. This Notice explains the limited data we process and why.

1. OUR PRIVACY PRINCIPLE
   Your terminal sessions, keystrokes, command output, command history, files, and
   settings stay on your device. There is no first-party cloud storage and no
   first-party hosted AI service. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or use the
   contents of your terminals, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking.

2. WHAT WE PROCESS
   a) Update checks. The Software may contact our update service to see whether a
      newer version is available. This request necessarily reveals your IP address,
      the app version, and basic platform information (e.g., operating system) to the
      update host. We use this only to deliver updates; we do not use it to build a
      profile of you.
   b) Pro purchases (Pro tier only). If you buy a Pro license, we and our payment
      processor collect the information needed to take payment and issue and validate
      your license — such as your email address, billing details, and a license
      record. Card/payment details are handled by our payment processor, not stored
      by us. We retain the purchase and license record to provide the Pro license,
      support you, and meet tax and accounting obligations.
   c) Support correspondence. If you contact us, we process the information you choose
      to send (e.g., your email address and message) to respond.

3. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT
   We do not collect terminal contents, keystrokes, command history, file contents,
   or peering traffic. Cross-machine peering (Pro) connects your own devices directly
   over your local/private network; that traffic does not pass through us.

4. SERVICE PROVIDERS (SUB-PROCESSORS)
   We share the limited data above only with providers that help us operate:
   - an update/distribution host (to serve update checks and downloads); and
   - a payment processor (to take Pro payments and manage licenses).
   These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and safeguards.

5. LEGAL BASES AND PURPOSES
   We process the data above to provide and secure the Software, deliver updates,
   fulfill Pro purchases and licenses, respond to you, and comply with law.

6. DATA RETENTION
   We keep update-check logs only as long as needed to operate and secure the
   service. We keep Pro purchase and license records for as long as needed to provide
   the license and to meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, then delete or
   anonymize them.

7. YOUR CHOICES AND RIGHTS
   Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or
   port your personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To make a
   request, email privacy@termflow.app; we will respond as required by applicable law.

8. SECURITY
   We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited
   data we process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. CHILDREN
   TermFlow is a developer tool not directed to children, and we do not knowingly
   collect personal data from children under 13 (or the age of digital consent in
   your jurisdiction).

10. INTERNATIONAL USERS
    We operate from the United States. If you use the Software from outside the U.S.,
    the limited data described above may be processed in the U.S. and other countries
    where we or our providers operate.

11. CHANGES
    We may update this Notice for new versions of the Software. The current version is
    published at https://termflow.app/privacy (when available); material changes will
    be noted there.

12. CONTACT
    PMT Labs LLC, Texas, USA — privacy@termflow.app.
